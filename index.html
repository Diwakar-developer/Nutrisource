Easy Microgreens Growing Guide

Follow our simple steps to grow your own microgreens at home in just days

1

Choose Your Seeds

Select from our premium organic microgreen seeds like mustard, sunflower, or fenugreek.

2

Prepare Your Tray

Fill a shallow tray with 2 inches of organic potting mix or coconut coir, leveling the surface.

3

Sow the Seeds

Evenly scatter seeds across the surface (about 1-2 tablespoons per tray), pressing gently into soil.

4

Cover & Water

Cover with another tray to create darkness for 2-3 days, misting lightly twice daily.

5

Harvest & Enjoy

After 7-14 days when first true leaves appear, snip just above soil level with clean scissors.
